Simple setup

Up and running in minutes

No complex configuration. No IT department needed. Just open your application and start managing your center.

1
Add Your Students

Enter students manually or bulk-import from a CSV file. Set subjects, classification, and weekly schedule.

2
Configure Your Schedule

Set open days, assign staff, and let the center calendar populate automatically from student schedules.

3
Start Each Session

Students check in, get seated, and begin studying. The live view shows you who's active and what they're working on.

4
Review & Report

End-of-day reports, attendance logs, and library status — always up to date, always printable.

Everything you need

Powerful tools for modern learning centers

From the first check-in to the final report, StdyTime handles it all — so you can focus on what matters most: the students.

👥
Student Management

Maintain rich student profiles with subjects, classifications, contact info, schedules, and QR-code student cards. Export & import via CSV.

🪑
Live Seating & Check-In

See who's in the room in real time. Assign students to seats and track active class sessions with a beautiful visual classroom layout.

📅
Center Calendar

Weekly schedule view shows every student's session at a glance — color-coded by type (Early Learner, Primary Instruction, Virtual) for instant clarity.

🗓️
Staff Scheduling

Monthly staff calendar lets you assign staff to open days, mark center closures, and print or export PDF schedules for your whole team.

📚
Library Book and Materials Tracking

Manage your center's book and material inventory. Log loans, track returns, and never lose a book or a tablet again with the built-in library and inventory management module.

📊
Reports & Analytics

Generate attendance reports, student progress snapshots, and center utilization stats. Print or save as PDF in one click.
